There's something about the act of sharing music with others that cannot be put to words. That muscle has atrophied for me. I used to play shows, organize music events, put music up online, jam with friends. Not so much of that lately. Just fiddling on the guitar and learning pieces.

There's still that desire to share, so I'm trying to do this by creating a sort of music corner where I'll put up recordings of pieces I'm learning and perhaps some of the fiddling too. Music is still a part of me, not just an atrophying muscle
